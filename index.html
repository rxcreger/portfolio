About Me


My journey to UX design started with a Bachelors degree in graphic design from UC Davis. After graduation, I spent seven years as a community manager at 99designs, a two sided marketplace where clients come to work with graphic designers from all over the world. I cared deeply about this community of designers, advocating for their needs to the Product teams and leading initiatives to help them succeed on the platform. This made me realize I wanted it to be my job to design the changes our users were asking for. I enrolled in the Bloc apprenticeship to update my skills, learning the latest design practices from seasoned professionals.
